This task combines the filter() and map() functions together on a single stream of data. Your goal is to take a list of mvp's, and filter out those whose salary was less than the salary parameter. You will then need to map to a list of the mvp's names.

The filter function needs a lambda or method reference, that returns true if the mvp.getSalary() is less than the salary parameter.

The map function needs a lambda or method reference, where the input is an instance of MVP and the output is the mvp's name.
